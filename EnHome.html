About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Hefei University of Technology, supervised by Prof. Kui Yu. I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Hefei University of Technology School of Computer Science and Technology in June 2020.

My research interests mainly include Knowledge Graph, Meta-learning, Natural Language Processing, etc.
